1) After each player places 3 pieces, they must move an existing piece to an adjacent empty square instead of placing a new one 
2) If it is your turn, you have 3 pieces, and one of your pieces is in the center square indice 4, your move has to be either win the game of move away from the center square
3) I added two helper functions . countPieces(squares, player)that counts how many pieces a player has on th eboard and isAdjacent(from, to) that checks if two squares are next to each other by converting the square indices to row/column cordinates and checking if they are at most 1 step apart in any direction even the diagonal 
4) I added  a new state variable to Board component, const [selectedSquare, setSelectedSquare] = useState(null);.
this tracks which piece the current player has selected to move. It is null when no piece is selected and a number 0-8 when a piece is selected.
5) I also modified handleClick to handle two different situations: If current player has less than 3 pieces on the board, the game works exactly like tic-tac-toe and places a piece normally. If current player has 3 pieces on the board, the first click selects one of their pieces and the second click moves it to an adjacent empty square
6) I added center square rule. The function function centerRuleCheck checks if a move violate the center square rule. the function checks if the current player has a piece in the square 4, if the current player has 3 pieces total, the proposed move neither wins the game nor vacates the center square. if all three conditions are true the move is illegal and the selection resets.
